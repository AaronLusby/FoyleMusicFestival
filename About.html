About the Festival

Foyle Music Festival has been in the making since 2019, this festival will have live music artists each day followed by smaller local bands playing during the evening, free of charge to attend them.

There will be hot food stands along with drinks and free water dispensers to fill your bottles up to stay hydrated aswell. This festival is hoped to bring the Community stronger by allowing smaller bands to play and build their awareness to greater crowds of people.
